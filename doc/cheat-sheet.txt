How do I know why an IP was DROPed?

First take a look at:
logscanner/util/see-table-DROP.sh | less -S
The column 'LineOrTmstmp' will have either of 2 values: a line or a timestamp.
Only lines cached by rexeg with 'severity 0' will be seen.
That's why the IP was DROPed.

The timestamp comes from the eval thread.
When eval decides to drop it creates a timestamp that is used to flag all the lines in the HIST table that were in the EVAL table.
Get the timestamp from DROP and find the lines That have it in HIST.
Those were the lines that triggered the drop.

Example with line:
kitepilot@towplane:~$ logscanner/util/see-table-DROP.sh | grep '20.168.6.241'
Timestamp            Octect_1  Octect_2  Octect_3  Octect_4  ProcName                        LineOrTmstmp    Status

kitepilot@towplane:~$
